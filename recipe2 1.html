Burger

Equipment:

  • Pan
  • stove
  • Spoon

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Lettuce
  • tomatoes
  • Cheese
  • spices

Steps:

  1. Warm up the pan
  2. Toast the buns
  3. Cook the burger for 3 to 5 minutes
  4. Flip the burgers and cook for another 3 to 5 minutes
  5. When done cooking put it onto the buns
  6. Add your ingredients and enjoy.

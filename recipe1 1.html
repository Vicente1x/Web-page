Spaghetti

Equipment:

  • Pan
  • Drainer
  • Spoon

Ingredients:

  • Pasta
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • salt

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add salt put in 3 tablespoons of salt.
  3. Add the pasta.
  4. Cook the meat
  5. Stir the pasta.
  6. Drain the pasta.
  7. Put the meat in the pot with the pasta.
  8. Add the sauces.
  9. Put cheese on it and eat it.
